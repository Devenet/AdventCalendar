Advent Calendar /about/

What is this?

Advent Calender is a very light web application to show a photo and its legend per day before Christmas.

Itâ€™s written in PHP, use last web technologies, and proud to not support old versions of IE.

How to configure it?

Put your photos in the private/ folder, and name them with the number of the day you want to illustrate.
To add a title, a legend or a text on a day page, just rename calendar.example.json in calendar.json and add what you want to display.

You can edit settings.json to personalize Advent Calendar.

Oups, problems?

All days are shown before Christmas: Check the syntax in settings.json or update the year.

Photo is not displayed: Be sure your photo is correctly named, like 3.jpg or 12.jpeg.

Title, legend or text are not displayed: Check the syntax of your calendar.json file.

Day is shown in late or advance: Configure the timezone of your server.
